The florida trip

The Florida Keys are a chain of islands composed of coral and limestone, renowned for their world-class scuba diving opportunities and rich history. The islands offer a resort-like atmosphere, even though most residents live in concentrated communities it feels like a resort. Stretching over 180 dazzling miles, the Keys provide a unique blend of natural beauty and vibrant activities.

If you're passionate about marine biology or simply love swimming, this trip is perfect for you! From April 5th to the 11th, you'll stay in charming dormitories and local hotels. During the day, you'll dive into the crystal-clear waters to explore marine life while learning about marine biology through hands-on experience
